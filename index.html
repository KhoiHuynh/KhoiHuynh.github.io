About Me

Nice to meet you! If you're interested to know a little more about me, I'm a graduate from Carleton University with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science, specializing in Management & Business Systems. I also hold an MBA degree from Western Sydney University. With 7 years in the tech industry, I bring a diverse background that spans software development and product management across various sectors including B2C e-commerce SaaS apps, insurance-tech, and recruitment CRMs. Prior to transitioning into product management, I gained valuable experience as a software developer, contributing to projects in health tech, fintech, case management systems, and other client-facing applications.

Throughout my career, I've had the opportunity to work and study in multiple countries, which has shaped my adaptive mindset and honed my communication skills. I have a passion for exploring new places and experiencing different cultures. Whether it's discovering hidden gems for food in Vietnam, capturing moments with my Nikon D3400 + 35mm portrait lens, or staying active with sports, I enjoy embracing life's adventures.
